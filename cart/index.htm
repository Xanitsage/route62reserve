Thank you for visiting and shopping at Scepter and Sword. Following are the terms and

Someone 21+ years old must be home to sign for your wine shipment. Amid Covid, our preferred carriers have adjusted their protocol for your safety and provide contactless delivery.

DOMESTIC SHIPPING POLICY

All orders are processed within 2-3 business days. Orders are not shipped or delivered on
