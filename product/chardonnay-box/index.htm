SHIPPING POLICY

Thank you for visiting and shopping with Route 62 Reserve. Below are the terms and conditions that govern our shipping process.

An adult over the age of 18 must be available to receive and sign for the wine delivery, as per South African law. Our courier partners may offer contactless delivery options when possible.

NATIONAL SHIPPING POLICY

  • Shipment processing time

All orders are processed within 2–3 business days. We do not ship on weekends or public holidays.

If order volume is high, shipping may be delayed slightly. You’ll be contacted via email or phone if a delay is expected.

  • We currently ship across South Africa. If you live in a remote area, delivery may take slightly longer.

SHIPPING RATES & DELIVERY ESTIMATES

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your delivery address and courier service selected.

Courier Options & Delivery Times:

Standard Courier: 3–5 business days – R90 (Free for orders over R800)

Express Delivery: 1–2 business days – R150

Same-day (Cape Town only): by 5pm – R200

Please note: Delivery times may vary during holidays or due to unforeseen circumstances.

ORDER TRACKING

You will receive an email with your tracking number once your order has been dispatched. Tracking will be active within 24 hours.

CUSTOMS, DUTIES & TAXES

All prices are in South African Rands and include VAT. Route 62 Reserve is not liable for any additional customs charges if shipping outside of South Africa becomes available in the future.

INTERNATIONAL SHIPPING

We currently only ship within South Africa. Please contact us directly for special arrangements or trade enquiries.

DAMAGES & RETURNS

Your satisfaction is our top priority. While we ensure safe packaging, Route 62 Reserve is not liable for any damage during transit. If your order arrives damaged, please contact the courier to lodge a claim.

Be sure to retain all packaging and damaged items when submitting your claim.
